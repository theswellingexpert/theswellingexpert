Who We Serve

Who Can Benefit

Our virtual programs are designed for a wide range of swelling-related conditions, from post-surgical recovery to chronic lymphatic concerns and lifestyle-related swelling.

Post-Surgical Swelling

Orthopedic, reconstructive, and aesthetic surgery recovery. Hip/knee replacements, BBL, tummy tuck, mastectomy, and more.

Lipedema

Chronic bilateral leg swelling and pain. Conservative management strategies to reduce discomfort and slow progression.

Venous Insufficiency

Chronic venous swelling, varicose veins, and phlebolymphedema. Supportive approaches for lasting comfort and relief.

Travel & Lifestyle Swelling

Frequent flyers, sedentary professionals, and active individuals experiencing swelling from daily life patterns.

Post-Aesthetic Swelling

Swelling following Botox, fillers, facial procedures, and med spa treatments. Supportive recovery techniques to ease discomfort.

Orthopedic Recovery

Joint replacement, sports injury, and orthopedic surgical recovery with swelling management as a core component.
